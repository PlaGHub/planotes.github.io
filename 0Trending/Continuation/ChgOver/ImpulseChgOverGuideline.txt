Change Over May be formed by:
* one long candle or a series of consecutive candles in same direction, in either case the 
* changeover candle must form bullish/bearish engulfing pattern and preceded by clear down 
  or up direction folow by a period of consolidation.
* Engulfing must enulf the entire consolidation phase's range, and
* higher probability changeover setup usually associated with price on the correct side of 
  15M's MA1 and preferrably H1's MA1 as well, as well as
* having air pocket for price to run
