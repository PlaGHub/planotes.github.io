Works best and prevalent in a clear trend
Normally preceded by a short term strong directional move follow by a period of consolidation
the waterfall drip towards the SR line of consolidation phase
price accelerate after breaking the SR line

Waterfall characteristic
1. a series of short 15M candle
2. waterfall candles have short or no wick
3. they all have same colour
4. they make lower lows and highs (downside move) or higher lows and highs (upside move)
