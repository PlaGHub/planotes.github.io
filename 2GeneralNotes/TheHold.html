Holding a trade

Day trading capturing pips from daily price action based on high probability setups

Immediately after enter trade (trending environment)

  • For a breakout trade, is there an immediate follow through in M1 or tight consolidation, if not consider closing out position
  • if tight consolidation is unfolding, then price action in M1 must reflect the follow:
    • In M1 chart, the 2 consolidation candles following breakout candle(s) must not retrace more than Fib328, the more shallow the better
    • In M1 chart, the 2 consolidation candles following breakout candle(s) must not make lower closes (for uptrend) or higher closes (for downtrend)
  • Confirm that price action is still valid for the setup
    • Are a series of M1 highs and lows performed in accordance to direction of trade
    • Are a series of M5 highs and lows performed in accordance to direction of trade
    • Has any SR line or inside_X been broken that invalidate the trade
  • Is the trade unambiguously supported and sustained by news?
    • If it is, have more conviction on holding the position.
    • A trade unambiguously supported by news has at least 2x long 15M candles

Letting price run (trending environment)

  • See "confirm price action list" to continue monitor validity of trade
    • Close out position if price action invalidate the direction of trade
  • On continuation of breakout, ensure that retrace is shallow (FibR23.6%)
  • Look for deceleration of price movement in M1 for scalping
  • Look for deceleration of price movement in M5 and M15 for trading breakout
  • Respect the major SR lines on first touch
  • Respect the minor SR lines during quiet period
  • In a clear trend (H1), use Fibonacci levels to set exit for countertrend position
  • when trading breakout of tight consolidation range, use duration of consolidation to evaluate depth of breakout
  • Place a time limit on holding a trade and no overnight holding
    • Close out position if any of the above are true

In a ranging environment, the M1 price action behave differently, M1 price movement is not as directional and contain more whipsaws

V-shape reversal tend to be more prevalent as price does not need to work off momentum cf. with trending environment

Also, pips gain from breakouts in ranging environemnt are small and fakeouts more likely, it may be better to fade than to break in a ranging environment
