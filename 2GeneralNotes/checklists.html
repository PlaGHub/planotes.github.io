Checklists for painting right side of chart

Day trading capturing pips from daily price action based on high probability setups

Start of Tokyo

  • Overnight PA
  • Pivot configuration
  • Look for potential xJPY moves into air pocket, in direction of trend, backed by news
  • Look for oppotunities to trade consolidation of overnight PostHighVol price action
  • Scan M1 for discernible patterns during active phase to trade break into air pockets
  • avoid trading off pivot if overnight price action is ambiguous
  • avoid trading off pivot when overnight price action has been invalidated
    • i.e., a series of extremes in (M1?), M5 and M15 charts counter to direction of trade setup

Start of London

  • ASR range
  • Look for trend, setup, air pocket and moves backed by news
  • The NY session's Post HighVol follow through consolidation
  • Beware of fakeout PA
  • Look for BIR PA (Look for 15M candle engulfing previous 15Ms)
  • Look for sneakout (long 15M candle just break out of ASR with no wick)
  • ensure that a series of extremes in (M1?), M5 and M15 charts confirm the direction of trade setup
  • High probability patterns (into air pocket)
    • the bowl with right half of bowl move in direction of trend
    • Spring, waterfall and 3Crow in direction of trend
    • FlatbaseT in direction of trend
    • Compressed 15M's MA12 in direction of trend
    • H1's MA1 bounce in direction of trend

Trading context

  • Market sentiment [risk on | risk off | neutral]
  • Short term technical [trending | consolidating | distributing]
  • Impending major economic release [holding mode | free flowing]
  • Market phase [active | quiet]
  • Action [breakout | fade | stay out]

Continue off pivot

  • Avoid trading off pivot against strong short term momentum and when price action does not support direction of trade
  • In trending environment, pivot must set SR line in support of higher timeframe trend
    • it must set lower high for down trend and higher low for uptrend
    • Price may crash through and retest pivot during Tokyo active phase
    • or price may bounce off it if price has already cleared pivot prior to start of Tokyo session

BIR Engulfing

  • Must unfold during active phase of trading session
  • 15M engulfing candle (or series of candles) must completely engulf at the close with small wick
  • Close of engulfing candle must be inside previous trading session's range at the start of new session
  • Setup is strongest in the direction of the trend or neutral environment
  • Beware of whipsaw when trade against direction of the trend

Reverse off pivot

  • Avoid trading off pivot against strong short term momentum and when price action does not support direction of trade
  • In a ranging environment, pivot may reinforce existing SR lines where price could fade of it
  • Avoid trading countertrend off pivot unless there's compelling reason to do so

ASR Breakout

  • Long wick, deep retrace and price action of extremes against trading direction (e.g., higher lows for shorting) invalidates the breakout
  • Best breakout setup is long no wick 15M candle close just outside ASR (the sneakout)
  • Best breakout setup when move in direction of trend into air pocket
  • Must wait for long candle to close outside ASR, do not anticipate a break out, especially in a neutral or counter trend direction
  • In M1 chart, the 2 consolidation candles following breakout candle(s) must not retrace more than Fib328, the more shallow the better
  • In M1 chart, the 2 consolidation candles following breakout candle(s) must not make lower closes (for uptrend) or higher closes (for downtrend)

High probability pattern into air pocket

  • The following patterns should preceed a breakout (preferrably sneakout) candle into air pocket
    • The bull flag
    • Highly stretched Head Shoulder
    • FlatbaseT
    • The bowl
    • Zig-Zag
    • 3 Crow and waterfall
    • Double and triple top/bottom

Fading a move

  • Work with direction of trend unless there's compelling reason to work against it
  • avoid fading against strong momentum off minor SR line or inside extreme of a candle
  • Outside candle that fully engulfing a series of smaller candles are best signal to fade, conversely double tops are unreliable signals
  • First touch of a strong SR line or trendline by an exhaustive candle is a high probability fade trade

Exhaustion PA

  • The plunge (V-shaped reversal is the norm)
  • The deceleration (gradual reversal with prolonged consolidation is expected)

Post High Volatility

  • Depth of retracement
  • Length of consolidation
